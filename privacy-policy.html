Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Quality Care Services arranges care and domestic support in families' homes in Kota. That work depends on trust, so this page states plainly what happens to the information you share with us. It describes what we actually do — not what is merely permitted.

What you share with us

When you submit the inquiry form on this website, you provide:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your location or area in Kota
  • The service you are asking about
  • When you need support
  • Any additional details you choose to write (optional)

If you call us or message us on WhatsApp instead, we receive whatever you choose to tell us in that conversation, along with your phone number.

Why we use it

We use this information for one purpose: to understand your requirement and contact you about it. We do not sell it, rent it, or share it for advertising.

Who else handles it

This website is a simple static site, so a few external services are involved in delivering it and receiving your inquiry. Being specific matters more than being reassuring:

  • Web3Forms — processes the inquiry form submission and delivers it to our business email. Your form contents pass through their service.
  • Google Analytics — measures how the website is used, such as which pages are viewed and whether visitors tap Call, WhatsApp, or the form. We do not send your name, phone number, address, or message text to Google Analytics.
  • Google (Apps Script) — serves the customer reviews shown on our homepage. This sends no information about you.
  • Netlify — hosts the website and serves its pages.
  • Cloudflare CDN — delivers the icon font used in the page design.

Each of these operates under its own privacy terms.

Cookies and measurement

Google Analytics sets cookies to understand website usage. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings; the website remains usable if you do. We do not use advertising or retargeting cookies.

How long we keep it

Inquiry details remain in our business email and our records for as long as needed to respond to you and to maintain a record of the service arranged.

Your choices

You can ask us to tell you what information we hold about you, correct it if it is wrong, or delete it. Contact us using the details below and we will action reasonable requests.

Children

This website is intended for adults arranging care. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children. Where a service concerns a child, the arrangements are made with the parent or guardian.

Changes to this policy

If our practices change, we will update this page and change the date shown above.
