Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions families often ask before arranging home care or domestic support.

How quickly can you arrange staff in Kota?

For urgent requirements, we can arrange staff within 24 to 48 hours in most cases. For standard requirements, we typically need 2 to 3 days to match the right person to your specific needs. We understand that some situations can't wait — a patient being discharged, a sudden need at home. Call us and we'll do our best to help quickly.

Are your caregivers and staff verified?

Yes, all staff members go through a verification process before placement. This includes identity verification, background checks, and assessing their experience and suitability for the specific duty. We share relevant details with the family before the service begins. We take this seriously because we know they'll be in your home, with your family.

Do you provide 24-hour attendants?

Yes, we provide both 12-hour and 24-hour attendant services. For 24-hour duty, we can arrange either a single attendant with rest breaks or two attendants working in shifts, depending on the patient's condition and family preference.

Which areas in Kota do you serve?

We provide services across Kota including Talwandi, Vigyan Nagar, Mahaveer Nagar, Kunhari, Landmark City, and other areas within the city. For locations slightly outside Kota, please contact us to confirm availability.

How are service charges decided?

Charges depend on several factors: the type of service required, whether it is medical or domestic, duty hours (12-hour or 24-hour), the patient's condition and level of care needed, and whether the arrangement is temporary or long-term. We discuss all charges clearly before the service starts so there are no surprises.

Can I hire staff for a short period or temporary need?

Yes, we provide both temporary and long-term staffing solutions. Many families hire attendants for post-hospitalization recovery periods of 15 to 30 days. We also arrange short-term domestic help for specific situations like family functions, travel periods, or medical emergencies.

What healthcare services do you provide at home?

Our healthcare services include home nursing for medical care like injections, dressings, and vitals monitoring; elder care for daily assistance with hygiene and mobility; mother care for post-delivery recovery and newborn support; child care and baby care for newborns and young children; ICU care at home for high-dependency patients; patient attendant services for hospital or home supervision; and post-hospitalization recovery support.

Do you provide Mother Care services after delivery?

Yes, we provide Mother Care services for women before and after delivery. This includes assistance with recovery, daily care, help with feeding routines, and support with newborn care so the mother can rest and recover comfortably. Our caregivers are experienced in handling post-delivery needs and can work on a day or 24-hour basis. We know the first few weeks after delivery can be overwhelming — having someone experienced to help can make a real difference.

Do you provide caregivers for newborns and young children?

Yes, we provide Child Care and Baby Care services with caregivers who are experienced in handling newborns, infants, and young children. This includes feeding support, diaper changes, sleep routines, and general supervision so parents can have peace of mind while at work or attending to other responsibilities. We understand that leaving your baby with someone is not easy — we try to send caregivers who are gentle and experienced, so you can feel more at ease.

How do I book your services?

You can contact us by calling +91 8302455482, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling the inquiry form on this page. Share your requirement, location, and when you need the service. Our team will call you to understand the details, discuss options, and guide you through the next steps.
